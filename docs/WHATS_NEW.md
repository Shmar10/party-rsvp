### How It Works

The **Event Description** field is a text box where you can write 1-2 lines describing your event. This text appears just below the event name on your RSVP webpage.

**Examples:**
- "Join us for an unforgettable evening of music, drinks, and great company."
- "Let's celebrate this milestone together!"
- "Help us send off Shawn in style - bring your swimsuit and party spirit!"

### Pro Tip

Keep it concise and inviting. This is the first thing guests read after seeing the event name, so make it count!
